Expert PF and ESIC Compliance Services in Pune and PCMC

The laws that govern employee provident funds and state insurance are designed to give workers a safety net for when they need it most. Unfortunately, even small mistakes in calculations or following the rules can lead to legal issues and financial penalties for a company. Luckily, MD Consultancy can help you navigate these complex laws, providing expert advice and support across all of your locations in India. We're here to be your partner in building a culture of strong corporate governance.

Our Services! What We Do.

  • We'll make sure your company is properly registered and any amendments are taken care of for the Employees Provident Fund and State Insurance Acts.
  • We generate sub-code numbers for all of your branches across India, ensuring your business stays compliant no matter where you operate.
  • We handle generating monthly contribution challans, including for any expat employees.
  • Our team ensures timely remittance of all contributions, taking the hassle out of keeping up with payments.
  • We help employees get their ESI numbers and insurance cards, providing the necessary coverage they need.
  • We also help issue certificates of coverage from the EPFO to ensure all employees are protected.
  • We prepare and submit all statutory registers and returns according to statutory periodicity, ensuring your business stays on top of its reporting requirements.
  • We make it easy for employees to transfer in, transfer out, withdraw, and make pension claims.
  • We'll even help employees access ESI benefits, such as accident claims and medical coverage.
  • Our team can arrange an employee help desk to answer any questions they might have. We coordinate with our clients to handle inspections by statutory authorities and ensure your business stays compliant.
  • We're even available to attend and contest quasi-judicial enquiries on behalf of management, handling everything from EPF to ESI matters.

Contract Labour Compliances

Hiring contract labor through contractors and agencies is a common practice in many countries and industries, for jobs that require different levels of skill, including skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled work. It's a trend that's now seen worldwide, and with the market-driven economy, organizations sometimes need to employ temporary skilled or unskilled workers to meet occasional surges in demand

However, employers need to be careful when hiring contract labor. They should ensure that the contractor is not cheating the contract worker and that they receive all the social security and other benefits to which they're entitled. Employers have a responsibility to make sure that the contract worker is not being taken advantage of.

If an employer fails to comply with these requirements, they will be held responsible for any benefits payable to the contract workers. So, it's important for organizations to follow labor laws and protect the rights of their contract workers.

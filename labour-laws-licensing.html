Labour Laws Licensing

Starting a new business in India is an exciting venture, but it's important to follow the necessary legal procedures. Depending on the type and location of your business, you may need to obtain specific licenses, registrations, or permissions to operate legally.

It's important to note that labor laws in India are subject to both the central and state governments, with legislation enacted at both levels. As labor law experts, MD Consultancy can guide you through the diverse compliance requirements and help you identify the specific licenses, registrations, or permissions your business may need. We'll work with you to obtain all the necessary permissions, ensuring that your business plans are set up for success in India.

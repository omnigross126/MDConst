Vision

Our vision at MD Consultancy Services is to be recognized as the leading provider of labor law expertise and support, trusted by businesses and individuals across all industries and geographies. We aspire to create a world where workplace rights and responsibilities are fully understood, respected, and upheld, and where all parties are able to thrive in a culture of mutual respect, fairness, and cooperation.

Mission

At MD Consultancy Services, our mission is to provide expert guidance and support to our clients in navigating the complex and ever-evolving landscape of labor law. We are committed to delivering personalized and practical solutions that empower employers and employees alike, ensuring fairness, equity, and compliance for all.


SHORT HISTORY

In 2001, Late Rajendra M. Dhawale founded MD Consultancy Services, a consulting firm that specializes in labor law. His vision was to create a company that would continue his legacy and provide expert guidance to workers and employers alike. He built the foundation for MD Consultancy Services on the principles of integrity,

Late Rajendra M. Dhawale was an exceptional lawyer who dedicated his life to practicing labor law. He was a renowned expert in the field & provided invaluable counsel to his clients for over two decades. His unparalleled commitment to the cause of labor rights earned him a reputation as a trusted advisor and a formidable force in the legal community. His unwavering dedication to his clients and his passion for his work earned him numerous accolades and recognition.

MD Consultancy Services has since grown to become a leading consultancy firm, providing comprehensive and innovative solutions to its clients. Its success is a testament to Late Rajendra M. Dhawale’s vision, dedication, and legacy. He may have passed on, but his impact on the legal profession and the lives of those he served will always be remembered.
